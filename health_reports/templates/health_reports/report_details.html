About EcoReport

Empowering communities to protect the environment

EcoReport is a community-driven platform that bridges the gap between citizens and environmental authorities. We believe that everyone has a role to play in protecting our planet, and we've made it easier than ever to report and track environmental issues.

Our mission is to create cleaner, greener communities by empowering individuals to take action and holding authorities accountable for swift resolutions.
